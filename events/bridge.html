Bridge Making Competition

Participants will build a bridge using wooden ice cream sticks and Fevicol. The competition aims to test the strength and efficiency of the bridge design based on the load-to-weight ratio.

Rules

  • Team Size: Maximum of 4 members.
  • Materials Provided:
    • - 100 wooden ice cream sticks
    • - Fevicol
  • Modification Allowed: Sticks may be cut or altered as needed.
  • Bridge Dimensions:
    • - Length: 48–56 cm
    • - Width: 12–16 cm
    • - Height: 12–16 cm
  • Time Limit: 2 hours and 30 minutes.

Testing Criteria

  • The bridge will be placed over an arena with two wooden columns representing landmasses.
  • Bridges will be evaluated based on the highest load-to-weight ratio.
  • Prizes will be awarded to first, second, and third place winners.

Additional Notes

  • Each team must designate one group leader responsible for coordination.
  • Names on certificates will match registration form entries.
  • All required materials will be provided on the spot.
