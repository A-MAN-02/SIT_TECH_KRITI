Sand Rover Challenge

The **Sand Rover Challenge** is an exciting hands-on event where teams compete to design and build their own mini rovers capable of navigating tough sand terrain. Participants will use innovation and engineering principles to create functional prototypes and race them through obstacles. Test your rover's durability, speed, and design in this thrilling competition!

Rules

  1. Teams must construct a working sand rover using given materials.
  2. Each rover must complete the obstacle course within the stipulated time.
  3. Judging criteria include speed, stability, and innovative design.
